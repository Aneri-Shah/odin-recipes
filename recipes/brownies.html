Description:

This brownie recipe results in a delicious, gooey, chocolatey delight, boasting a triple chocolate base that would pair beautifully with ice-cream!

You'll find this recipe immensely simple to follow, very beginner friendly, and incredibly quick to whip up when the craving hits!

Ingredients:

  • 200g dark choc
  • 200g butter
  • 3 large eggs
  • 275g caster sugar
  • 100g flour
  • 50g cocoa powder
  • 100g dark choc
  • 100g white choc
  • 100g milk choc

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 160 degrees
  2. Melt butter and dark chocolate together
  3. Whisk together eggs and caster sugar
  4. Mix chocolate with the egg mixture
  5. Once combined, sift cocoa powder and the flour and fold together
  6. Fold in chocolate chunks
  7. Put into tray
  8. Bake for 25-30 minutes
